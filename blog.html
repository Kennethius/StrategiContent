BLOG

Having a blog on your website is, by far, the simplest and most effective way of communicating your marketing message.

When useful and relevant articles are published, site visitors develop more trust in your company. And, gain deeper insight into your products-services, problem-solving and expertise.

Blogging’s Advantages

  • B2B blogs generate 60% more monthly leads than websites without
  • For B2C, it’s an 80% upswing
  • Builds greater brand awareness, visibility and credibility
  • Make customer buying decisions easier, based on familiarity

    • In contrast, a site lacking any engagement with site visitors appears to be neglected – like an abandoned building. How often have you visited one of those?

    To be easily found, and to generate website traffic, blog posts should be keyword-optimized. As the pages of quality content accumulate, search engines rate your site higher – which attracts more traffic (readers): More qualified readers = more leads.

    Planning your Company Blog Begins with a discussion of your objectives followed by my standard writing process, adapted for blogging:

  • Company-industry research
  • Marketing interview
  • Developing customer profiles and topic streams
  • Generating publicity for blog’s launch

    • Blog post Writing: 2 Choices

    1) Based on the subject matter matrix, I write the specified number of keyword-optimized blog posts. Other media may also be added, if applicable.

    2) For companies who prefer to author their own blog posts, I instruct that person on blog principles and Web writing’s style and punctuation. They may also consult me, if necessary.

    Analytics Blog post traffic and performance in the search rankings will be tracked to determine if/where marketing opportunities exist.
